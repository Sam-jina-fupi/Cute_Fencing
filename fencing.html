Fencing Products

ChainLink

Chain-link fences are made of interwoven galvanized or coated steel wires, creating a diamond pattern. They are durable, affordable, and provide a clear view of the surroundings. Chain-link fences are commonly used for residential, commercial, and industrial applications.

Wooden Poles

Wooden fences are made from different types of wood, such as cedar, pine, or redwood. They offer a natural and classic look, providing privacy, security, and a warm aesthetic appeal. Wooden fences can be customized in various styles, including picket, privacy, or split rail fences.

Precast Poles

Precast poles are pre-made concrete or metal poles that are designed to provide structural support for fences. They are commonly used for chain-link fences, wire mesh fences, or as posts for attaching other fencing materials. Precast poles offer durability, strength, and stability for the overall fence structure.

HT Wires

High-tension (HT) wires are strong and durable wires that are typically used in conjunction with posts or poles to create an electric or non-electric fencing system. HT wires are stretched tightly between the posts to provide a secure barrier. They can be electrified to create an electric fence, which serves as an effective deterrent for livestock or perimeter security.

Barbed Wire

Barbed wire fences are a type of fencing that incorporates strands of wire with sharp barbs or points attached at regular intervals. They are primarily used for security and containment purposes in various settings.

Other Concrete Products


Road Channels

Road channels, also known as cattle guards or livestock grids, are typically used in rural or agricultural settings to prevent livestock from crossing a road or driveway while allowing vehicles to pass freely. They consist of metal or concrete bars or grids embedded in the ground, creating a barrier that livestock are reluctant to cross.

Wall Coping

Wall coping refers to the protective covering or cap placed on top of walls or fences. It serves both functional and aesthetic purposes. Coping protects the wall from water damage, prevents erosion, and provides a finished appearance. It can be made of various materials, such as concrete, stone, metal, or even composite materials.

Slabs

Slabs, also known as concrete panels, are precast concrete elements used for fencing purposes. They can be utilized as a solid barrier or incorporated into the design of the fence to enhance privacy and security. Concrete slabs are sturdy, durable, and available in different sizes and finishes to complement various fencing styles.
